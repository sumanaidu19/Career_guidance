                Software engineering is a field dedicated to designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software systems systematically and efficiently. It involves the use of engineering principles to ensure that software is reliable, maintainable, and scalable. It encompasses a broad range of tasks and specializations, making it a critical and dynamic area within the technology sector. Software engineers use various programming languages, depending on the project requirements.
